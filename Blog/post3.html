'Mononoke: Karakasa' Reveals Supporting Cast

The official website for the Mononoke: Karakasa anime movie revealed ten supporting casts, theme song, key visual, and the first trailer. The movie is scheduled to open in Japanese theaters on July 26.

Cast:

  • Kitagawa: Kana Hanazawa (Kimetsu no Yaiba)
  • Botan Ootomo: Haruka Tomatsu (Darling in the FranXX)
  • Fuki Tokita: Youko Hikasa (Arifureta Shokugyou de Sekai Saikyou)
  • Awashima: Yuuko Kaida (Yakusoku no Neverland)
  • Mugitani: Yukana (Code Geass: Hangyaku no Lelouch)
  • Saburoumaru Tokita: Yuuki Kaji (Boku no Hero Academia)
  • Heiki Saga: Jun Fukuyama (Ookami to Koushinryou)
  • Sakashita: Daisuke Hosomi (Gatchaman Crowds)
  • Tenshi: Miyu Irino (Mob Psycho 100)
  • Hokuto Mizorogi: Kenjirou Tsuda (Jujutsu Kaisen)

Theme Song:

AiNA THE END (Kidou Senshi Gundam: Suisei no Majo Season 2, Kusuriya no Hitorigoto) is performing the theme song "Love Sick," which is previewed in the trailer below.

Staff:

  • Director: Kenji Nakamura (Kuuchuu Buranko)
  • Studio: EOTA
  • Character Designer: Yuuichi Takahashi (Vivy: Fluorite Eye's Song)
  • Music Composer: Taku Iwasaki (Bungou Stray Dogs)
