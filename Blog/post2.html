'Boku no Tsuma wa Kanjou ga Nai' Announces Additional Case, Staff

The official website for the television anime adaptation of Jiro Sugiura's Boku no Tsuma wa Kanjou ga Nai (My Wife Has No Emotion) manga revealed additional cast, staff, and a key visual on Thursday.

The anime is scheduled to premiere on July 2 at 11:00 p.m. on Tokyo MX, followed by MBS and BS Asahi.

Additional Cast:

  • Yoshino Aoyama (Bocchi the Rock!) as Akari Kosugi
  • Yuu Serizawa (Watashi no Oshi wa Akuyaku Reijou.) as Super Mina

Staff:

  • Color Design: Megumi Furuya (My Home Hero)
  • Art Director: Jirou Kouno (Gekkan Shoujo Nozaki-kun)
  • Director of Photography: Yoshie Itou (Collar x Malice Movie: Deep Cover)
  • Editing: Yuri Tamura (Dantalian no Shoka)
  • 3D Director: Bibury Animation CG, Beijing Xiele Art
  • Sound Director: Takumi Itou (3D Kanojo: Real Girl)
  • Sound Production: Half H.P Studio
  • Music: Kanade Sakuma (Akuyaku Reijou nanode Last Boss wo Kattemimashita), Hanae Nakamura (Majo to Yajuu), Natsumi Tabuchi (Adachi to Shimamura), Miki Sakurai (5-toubun no Hanayome)
  • Music Production: Nichion

Production Details:

Fumihiro Yoshimura (Tsundere Akuyaku Reijou Liselotte to Jikkyou no Endou-kun to Kaisetsu no Kobayashi-san) is helming the anime at Tezuka Productions. Mitsutaka Hirota (Kanojo, Okarishimasu) is handling the series composition and Zenjirou Ukulele (Kanojo mo Kanojo chief animation director) is designing the characters.

Manga Information:

Sugiura began serializing the romantic comedy manga in Comic Flapper in August 2019. Kadokawa published the seventh volume in October 2023. Seven Seas Entertainment licensed the manga in English in December 2020 and released the sixth volume on January 16.
